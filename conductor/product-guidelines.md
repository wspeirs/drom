# Product Guidelines - drom

## Documentation Style
- **Technical and Concise:** Prioritize brevity and precision in all documentation, focusing on providing clear, actionable information for experienced users and DevOps professionals.

## Design Philosophy
- **Minimalist:** Maintain a lean and fast toolset by focusing exclusively on essential task-running features.
- **Opinionated:** Provide strong, sensible defaults to minimize configuration overhead and provide a consistent user experience.

## UI/UX Principles (CLI)
- **Functional and Informative:** Use clear, color-coded status messages and detailed error reports to keep the user informed.
- **Friendly and Helpful:** Incorporate proactive suggestions and helpful corrections (e.g., "did you mean...") to enhance usability and reduce friction.
